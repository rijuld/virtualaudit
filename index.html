Virtual Audit

Virtual social audit of CRAWL and BOCW cash transfers made in Meghalaya 

Context

 The Government of Meghalaya announced CRAWL (Chief Minister’s ReliefAgainst Wage Loss) and BOCW (Building And Other Construction Workers Welfare Board) cash transfers as compensation for loss of income during the COVID-19 lockdown for unorganized sector and construction workers. TUR investigated the transfers through a sample survey and found irregularities in the payments. In a reply to an RTI query, the government has released the details of all the transfers on record.

The full list of transactions is here.

What can you do?

  • You can browse through the list of transactions to see if your name is in the list.
  • If yes, is the transaction recorded against your name correct?
  • If the record is incorrect, you can fill out this form (TODO) and TUR will take it up with the authorities
  • We will soon put up a search interface to make this process easier

    How to browse the list of transactions?

    • The list is a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet. You will need to open the list on a computer or a smartphone
    • On a computer, after you have opened the spreadsheet, you can press the Ctrl and F buttons on the keyboard together to open a textbox. In the textbox search for either your name or phone number to verify the details
    • On a smartphone, use the ‘Find’ menu to open the textbox where you can search your name or phone number
